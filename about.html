Celebrating 60 Years of Excellence

A Legacy of Innovation and Precision

Since 1965, Khurana Wire Drawing Dies has been a trusted name in the wire drawing industry. As we mark our 60th anniversary, we celebrate six decades of delivering exceptional quality, customer satisfaction, and groundbreaking innovation. Our journey reflects a commitment to precision and excellence, earning the trust of industries across the globe.

About Our Journey

Legacy Continues

Khurana Wire Drawing Dies was founded in 1965 by Late Avsharan Singh Khurana with a vision to create world-class wire drawing solutions. What began as a small endeavor has grown into a global enterprise, recognized for unmatched quality and reliability. Over the years, we have embraced innovation, adopted cutting-edge technology, and stayed true to our founder’s values of integrity and customer-centricity. At Khurana Wire Drawing Dies, we specialize in manufacturing premium Tungsten Carbide (TC) and Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD) dies. Serving industries such as construction, electronics, automotive, and more, we have become synonymous with quality and precision.
