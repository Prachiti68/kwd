About Us

Crafting Precision, Building Trust Since 1965

Khurana Wire Drawing Dies, established in 1965, is a renowned manufacturer and exporter of high-quality Tungsten Carbide (TC) and Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD) dies.

With over five decades of experience, the company has built a reputation for delivering products known for their strength and durability, catering to various industries worldwide.

Late Avsharan Singh Khurana laid the foundation of Khurana Wire Drawing Dies with a vision to deliver unmatched quality and innovation. His dedication and passion set the standard for excellence that drives us today.
